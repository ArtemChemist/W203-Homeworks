$$
Corr[aX+b,X]= \frac{Cov[aX+b,X]}{\sqrt{Var[aX+b]}*\sqrt{Var[X]}} = \frac{a*Cov[X,X]}{{|a|}*\sqrt{Var[X]}*\sqrt{Var[X]}}\\
= \frac{a}{|a|}*Corr[X,X] = \frac{a}{|a|}
$$
